THE BEST MOVIES :

The best 3 movies that have watched

1. Dune

Dune is a 2021 American epic science fiction film directed and co-produced by Denis Villeneuve. Set in the distant future, the film follows Paul Atreides as his family, the noble House Atreides, is thrust into a war for the deadly and inhospitable desert planet Arrakis.

2. Interstellar

Interstellar is a 2014 epic science fiction drama film directed by Christopher Nolan. Set in a dystopian future where Earth is suffering from catastrophic blight and famine, the film follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new home for mankind.

3. Madame Web

Madame Web is a 2024 American superhero film featuring the Marvel Comics character of the same name.
